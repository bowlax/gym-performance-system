What the app does

GymPerformance gives every WOLF member a personal record of their training. Your personal bests, your sessions, your progression - all in one place on your phone.

The app is built around the exercises you already do at the gym. Every PB exercise on the board is tracked automatically - log a session, and the app tells you if you've set a new personal best.

🏆

Personal Bests

Your current PB for every exercise, always up to date. See your board on your phone.

📋

Session Logging

Log what you did after each session. Quick, simple, and takes under two minutes.

📈

Progression

See how your PBs have moved over time with a clear chart for every exercise.

📅

Consistency

Track how often you're training each week and month at a glance.

How to download

The app is currently available via TestFlight, Apple's platform for beta apps. It takes about two minutes to set up.

  1. Download TestFlight Install the free TestFlight app from the App Store on your iPhone.
  2. Open the invite link Tap this link It will open in TestFlight automatically.
  3. Install GymPerformance Tap Install in TestFlight. The app will appear on your home screen.
  4. Set up your PBs When you first open the app, enter your current personal bests. You can always update these later.

Frequently asked questions

Is my data private?

For now. All your data is stored only on your phone. Nothing is sent to any server or shared with anyone. You can read the full privacy policy for details. If this changes we will let you know first.

What iPhone do I need?

Any iPhone running iOS 17 or later. That covers iPhone XS and newer from 2018 onwards.

Is it free?

Yes, completely free for Wolf Way of Life Fitness members.

What is TestFlight?

TestFlight is Apple's official platform for distributing apps before they're on the App Store. It's safe, free, and made by Apple. The app will move to the App Store in future.

Will I lose my data if the app updates?

No. Your sessions, personal bests, and history are all preserved when the app updates. Updates happen automatically through TestFlight.

Which exercises are tracked?

All the PB exercises on the gym board - including squats, deadlifts, bench press, overhead press, dumbbell exercises, chin-ups, cable row, plank, ski 500m, and bike. Conditioning exercises will be added in a future update.
